About us

Royal Removals Services is a removals company based in London specializing in packing, removals and self-storage services throughout the Eastern, Northern, Southern and Western parts of London. Whether you are moving within the UK or looking for international removals, you can always rely on us Royal Removals Services. We provide a comprehensive range of services to suit your needs including international removals and domestic moving services. In our opinion this is due to our basic ethos of combining fully trained staff, a modern fleet of vehicles and commitment to a fair price structure and customer
Royal Removals Services are young and ambitious company. Our team has successfully completed periodic training in many aspects of specializing in packing, removals and self-storage services. Our team consists of moving professionals conscientious and diligent experts. They are good trained and equipped with the latest tools and equipment to complete the work. Our command also knows London pretty well – and moving vans equipped with GPRS - so you can be confident that they will find shortest possible, most convenient route, when your move takes place.
